Google Sheets Instacart Assistant

A simple add-on that allows you to initiate an Instacart order from Google Sheets

Instructions

Download the free plug-in here

Setup Steps

  1. In your Google Sheet, ensure you have columns labeled "Item", "Quantity", and "Unit".
  2. Highlight the range of cells containing your shopping list data, including the headers.
  3. Click "Get Ingredients" to create a list based on your selection.
  4. Review the generated table in the sidebar to ensure accuracy.
  5. Click "Order with Instacart" to open your shopping list in Instacart and complete shopping!
