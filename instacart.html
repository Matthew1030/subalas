Instacart Assistant

A simple add-on that allows you to initiate an Instacart order from Google Sheets

Instructions

Download the free plug-in COMING SOON

Setup Steps

  1. Download the Google Sheets Add-On from the Google Workspace Marketplace
  2. Open your Google Sheet with your shopping list
  3. Click on "Extensions" in the menu bar
  4. Select "Instacart Assistant" from the dropdown menu
  5. Click "Open Sidebar" to start using the tool
