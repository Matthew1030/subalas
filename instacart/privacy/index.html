Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 05, 2025

Information Collection

The Instacart Assistant Add-On ("Add-On") collects and processes the following information:

  • Your Instacart account information (email address)
  • Your Google account information (email address)
  • Data from your Google Sheets (shopping lists and order details)

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Create and manage your Instacart orders
  • Improve the Add-On's functionality
  • Provide customer support

Permissions Required

The Add-On requires the following permissions:

  • Access to your Google Sheets to read shopping list data
  • Access to your Instacart account to create orders

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. Your data is:

  • Stored securely using Google's infrastructure
  • Transmitted using encrypted connections
  • Never shared with third parties except as required by law

We do not store any Google user data beyond the duration necessary to provide the service. All processing of your Google Sheet data (e.g., shopping list items, quantities, and units) occurs in memory and is cleared when the Instacart Assistant add-on is closed or the session ends. We do not persist this data to any database or server.

If you wish to delete your data processed by the add-on, you can do so by:

  • Closing the add-on sidebar, which clears all in-memory data.
  • Deleting or modifying the relevant data in your Google Sheet, as the add-on does not retain copies.
  • Uninstalling the add-on via the Google Workspace Marketplace, which removes its access to your Google account.

For any requests related to the deletion of data processed during your use of the add-on, please contact us at subalasllc@gmail.com. We will respond to such requests within a reasonable timeframe and ensure no residual data remains under our control.
